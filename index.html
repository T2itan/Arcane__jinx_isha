Arcane Season 1 cost approximately $80 million, while Season 2 escalated to around $100 million. Total spend reached at least 250 Million dollars.

Last role of Mira Furlan, who voices Babette. She passed away in January of 2021.

Imagine Dragons and J.I.D. did an official animated music video released just before the series.

Arcane is a steampunk action-adventure series created by Christian Linke and Alex Yee. Produced by Fortiche and Riot Games, it focuses on sisters Vi and Jinx amid the conflict between Zaun and Piltover.

The series received critical acclaim for its visuals and storytelling, becoming the first streaming series to win a Primetime Emmy for Outstanding Animated Program.

The first episode was completed roughly 4 years before the full production began.

Animators used real-life references for moves, then drew over 3D animations to get the unique polished look.

Fortiche uses special rigs that allow walking cycles to be shared across different character models.
